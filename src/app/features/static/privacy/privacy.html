Privacy.

Digital Sovereignty & Security Framework

Introduction

At AllMart, we view privacy not as a feature, but as a foundational right. Our systems are engineered to minimize data collection and maximize protection. This policy outlines how we handle the fragments of data required to operate our service.

01. Information Acquisition

We collect only what is strictly necessary to fulfill our contract with you. This includes:

  • Authentication Metadata
  • Shipping Coordinates
  • Order History
  • Browser User-Agents

02. Third-Party Integration

AllMart leverages specialized third-party services to enhance security:

"Financial data is never stored on our local servers. Payments are processed through Stripe’s encrypted infrastructure (AES-256)."
"Product assets and user images are managed via Cloudinary, ensuring optimized delivery and secure asset hosting."

03. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Upon account deletion requests, personal data is purged or anonymized within our primary database within 30 days, subject to legal record-keeping requirements.
